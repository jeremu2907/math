## Math
### Euler's Formula & Identity
Euler's identity
$$e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$$
links five fundamental mathematical constants — $0$, $1$, $e$, $i$, and $\pi$ — in a single, simple formula, showing a deep connection between exponentials, complex numbers, and trigonometry.
